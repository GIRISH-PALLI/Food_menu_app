DESCRIPTION

Paneer Biryani is a rich, flavorful, and aromatic rice dish made by layering spiced rice with marinated paneer (Indian cottage cheese) and cooking it to perfection. It's a vegetarian version of the classic biryani, where paneer serves as the main protein. The combination of fragrant basmati rice, aromatic spices, and the creamy paneer makes this dish a delicious and satisfying meal.
